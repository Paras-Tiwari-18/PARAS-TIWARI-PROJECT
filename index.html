We Create ,Job Creators

KIET E-Cell is a student body of Krishna Institute of Engineering and Technology, formed in 2014 with the aim to promote an entrepreneurial culture among students of technical streams, and encouraging the entrepreneurial minds of the students to convert their drop of an idea into an ocean of reality. Through its various activities and events, the Entrepreneurship Cell of KIET strives to enrich their knowledge in the domain of management and technology intervention, and overall ecosystem development for innovation and entrepreneurship.
